About the Department

Cutting-Edge Education

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is a powerhouse of technological advancement, academic excellence, and innovation. With a mission to cultivate future-ready professionals, the department offers a rigorous and industry-aligned curriculum that combines strong theoretical foundations with hands-on experience in cutting-edge technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, Cybersecurity, Blockchain, Cloud Computing, and Internet of Things (IoT).

Excellence in Research

Over the years, the CSE department has made remarkable strides, producing research that has been published in internationally reputed journals and presented at leading conferences such as IEEE, ACM, and Springer. Faculty members are actively involved in interdisciplinary research and have secured competitive grants from top research agencies and industry collaborators.

Students of the department have consistently excelled in both academic and co-curricular arenas—winning prestigious hackathons, coding competitions, and innovation challenges at national and international levels.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

Our aspiration is to become a leading center of excellence in the field of Computer Science and Engineering, distinguished by our commitment to education, research, and innovation. We aim to develop professionals who are not only technically proficient but also ethically grounded and socially responsible, equipped to make meaningful contributions toward sustainable global development.

In an era marked by rapid technological advancements, it is essential for our graduates to possess the versatility to adapt and excel across emerging and interdisciplinary fields.

Mission

  • Provide quality education in Computer Science & Engineering through innovative pedagogy, modern curriculum, and state-of-the-art infrastructure.
  • Build a strong computing foundation that enables students to adapt, specialize, and excel in associated domains.
  • Foster creativity, problem-solving, and leadership qualities through industry collaboration, research, and experiential learning.
  • Cultivate lifelong learning, ethical values, and social responsibility.

Our Objectives

Deliver high-quality, industry-relevant education in Computer Science & Engineering with a balance of theoretical foundations and practical applications.
Encourage faculty and students to pursue impactful research, publish in reputed journals, and contribute to innovation through projects, patents, and start-ups.
Build strong linkages with IT industries and research organizations to enhance curriculum relevance, internships, placements, and collaborative projects.
Develop technical competence, problem-solving, leadership, teamwork, communication, and ethical values among students to prepare them as responsible global professionals.
Cultivate adaptability and continuous learning skills, empowering graduates to succeed in higher studies, competitive exams, entrepreneurial ventures, and global careers.
