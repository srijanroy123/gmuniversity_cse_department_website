Profile

Mr. Santoshkumar Mahendrakar is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at GM Institute of Technology, Davangere, with more than 22 years of academic and research experience. He is currently pursuing his Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML), with research contributions in computational models, machine learning for agriculture (including SlimNet for foxtail millet growth analysis), and blockchain technologies.

Over the years, he has been actively engaged in teaching, curriculum design, and accreditation processes. He has strong expertise in NBA Accreditation, theory of computation, programming languages, and modern pedagogical practices.

Research & Teaching

Mr. Santoshkumar Mahendrakar's research interests span across Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Blockchain Technology, Automata Theory, and Smart Contracts. He has worked on applications of SlimNet for agricultural growth analysis and has developed simplified teaching models for blockchain, Ethereum, and automata theory.

He has taught a wide range of courses including:

  • Theory of Computation
  • Data structures and Applications
  • Programming Languages (C, Python, Java)
  • Blockchain Technology
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Agile Technology
  • Data mining and Data warehousing

He has designed question banks, schemes of valuation, and project-based learning activities using Revised Bloom's Taxonomy, fostering deeper student engagement.

Mr. Santoshkumar Mahendrakar believes in making learning accessible, analytical, and application-oriented.
