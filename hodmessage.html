Message from the HoD


Dr. Shivanagowda G M
Professor & Head, Department of Computer Science & Engineering

“At the heart of Computer Science lies the power of aptitude—the intellectual agility to learn, adapt, and innovate. In our rapidly changing world, this quality defines success.”


Welcome to the Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) at GM University, Davanagere. Our department is committed to nurturing talent, fostering innovation, and preparing students to excel in the rapidly evolving field of technology. In today’s digital era, Computer Science drives transformation across every sector—from Artificial Intelligence and Data Science to Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity, and the Internet of Things (IoT). At GMU, we ensure our students gain strong theoretical foundations along with hands-on experience through modern laboratories, real-world projects, and industry collaborations.

Our distinguished faculty are actively engaged in teaching, research, and consultancy, offering mentorship that bridges academia and industry. They are committed to instilling deep learning, analytical thinking, and adaptability—skills that empower our students to excel in competitive examinations such as GATE and GRE, and to make impactful contributions to the global academic and technology communities.

Beyond coursework, we nurture students’ innate capabilities by encouraging them to engage in research, innovation challenges, and entrepreneurship. Our learners actively participate in prestigious platforms such as the Smart India Hackathon (SIH), ACM Student Symposiums, ICPC, Google Summer of Code, and Microsoft Imagine Cup, gaining exposure to national and international problem-solving.

To sharpen their aptitude, they are trained on competitive coding platforms like LeetCode, Kattis, HackerRank, and Codeforces, building strong analytical and problem-solving skills. Students also collaborate with faculty in preparing research proposals for ISRO, DRDO, DST, and international agencies such as NASA, embedding a culture of scholarly engagement. I warmly invite you to explore what makes us unique. We are not only a department of engineers, but a hub where minds are honed, aptitudes are elevated, and futures are shaped.

– Dr. Shivanagowda G M
Professor & Head of Department
