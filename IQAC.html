IQAC & Quality Initiatives

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) actively engages in the Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) framework of GM University to strengthen teaching, learning, and academic governance. The department emphasizes quality enhancement through structured mentoring, data-driven decision-making, and continuous improvement practices.

Key Quality Initiatives

Mentorship Model

Each faculty member mentors students in a 1:30 ratio, ensuring personalized guidance, academic monitoring, and career support.

ERP-Enabled Quality Assurance

The department leverages the University ERP system for data visualization, performance tracking, and evidence-based decision-making. Insights generated from ERP data support decisions at the mentor, coordinator, and HoD levels, enhancing transparency and accountability.

OBE and Documentation

The IQAC coordinators maintain syllabus records, course outcome and program outcome mappings, feedback reports, placement statistics, and curriculum review data.

Continuous Improvement

Regular benchmarking against peer institutions, stakeholder consultations, and academic audits ensure alignment with AICTE/UGC regulations and industry requirements.

Through these initiatives, the CSE Department ensures that its quality practices are systematic, measurable, and forward-looking, creating an environment that supports student success and institutional growth.
